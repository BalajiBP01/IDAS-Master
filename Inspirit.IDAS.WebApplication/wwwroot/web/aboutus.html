WHO WE ARE

Inspirit Data Analytics Services provides insights to companies and assists in unlocking their unknown potential or hidden opportunities. by delivering top quality, business intelligence and analytic solutions under one umbrella.

​We believe in ‘no more guesswork and only actionable insights’ because effective analytics starts with trusted data.
Our core solutions in data analytics help our clients turn data-driven. This in turn aids the organisation to use data to make better decisions by giving them access to the data when they require it.

​We also understand that the word ‘Data’ scares companies because of the complexity associated with it, the cost per record and the data compliance (POPI). If you’re using your data to drive your business innovation, then there’s no room for error.
Data quality starts with compact data governance. It must be well-organized, accurate, relevant and understandable.

​At Inspirit, we’ll help create a product/solution that is easily accessible for small to medium organisations, not just blue chip companies. We allow you to leverage the power of data without fearing the exorbitant costs that might be linked with these products and services.

​With IDAS - we believe that Data can empower everyone by creating a Data as a Service model i.e. pay for what you need.
