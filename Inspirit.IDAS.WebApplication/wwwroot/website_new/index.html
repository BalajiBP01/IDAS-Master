About IDAS

Inspirit is an EOH enterprise (a division of the EOH Group) development company. Inspirit Data Analytics Services delivers digital transformation with data analytics from its ideation to execution. We are highly committed to sustainable enterprise development that cultivates our economy. Sustainability is an essential part of the Inspirit’s strategy since its inception.

In order to establish a strong foundation for the business, the following Vision, Mission and Core Values have been formulated to create the crux of the business.

The fundamental vision of the IDAS is to assist the South African market with profiled data solutions and inherently storing, protecting and connecting organizations with a digital environment.

The mission of IDAS can be summarised as follows:

• Utilize customer insights to build world-class products and service offerings.
• Focus our key resources on profitable growth areas.
• Increase the value of our brands and demonstrate return on investments.
• Lead with the highest ethical standards.

An organization’s core values and principles could be an endless list of good-to-hear items, but the following key attributes have been highlighted as vital points of growth for both, the upcoming company and its current and future clients:

• Put customers first.
• Act with integrity.
• Deliver excellence.
• Work Hard, Play Hard Approach.

Batch Trace

Leads Generation

This service generates marketing leads, suitable for high volume users – e.g. call centres, financial institutions and retailers – looking to increase their customer base. Data Cleaning

Data cleansing, data cleaning, or data scrubbing is the process of identifying, correcting or removing corrupt or inaccurate data from a database. The data cleaning solution takes any old and out-dated data submitted and return only the most current information.
