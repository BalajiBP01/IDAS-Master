About IDAS

Inspirit is an EOH enterprise (a division of the EOH Group) development company. Inspirit Data Analytics Services delivers digital transformation with data analytics from its ideation to execution. We are highly committed to sustainable enterprise development that cultivates our economy. Sustainability is an essential part of the Inspirit’s strategy since its inception.

EOH Group has partnered with Inspirit to grow the businesses and ensuring consistency and quality of your data for analytics. We have a broad range of tools and resources to provide highly visual representation of data as you want.

XDS, a Credit Information Bureau, is one of the major suppliers of business and consumer credit data to the South African Market and is also part of the group of EOH companies. This accredited source of data is the platform from which we build our data services.

Inspirit Data Analytics Services help companies to flourish by unlocking their hidden opportunities into valuable insights. We create core solutions for data analytics that help our clients turn data-driven. We aim to convert historical and real-time data into valuable insights that can work as an accurate forecasting tool for your business. At Inspirit Data Analytics Services, we believe in ‘no more guesswork and only actionable insights’ because effective analytics starts with trusted data.

Our ‘Aha’ moment: There is great power in data that is around us. More or less, data is the currency of the modern digital world. Meaningful Insights with valuable data is the first step to knowing your customers. It motivates us to think differently thereby inspiring us. The more insight we draw from data, the more rewarded we are, thereby becoming more empowered to affect those around us by the decisions we make.
